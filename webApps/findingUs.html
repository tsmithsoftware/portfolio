The reserve lies south east of Stourville, the county town of the ancient county of Dornam.

From the west, the reserve is reached via the A238 from Chatwick or Portugal. Coming from the east, use the road from Still (A696/A1). Free parking is available.

Or why not come by train? - Stourville station (0.5 miles away) is served by an excellent east-west service linking Prussia to Leicester and Birmingham.

Dogs: to safeguard wildlife, grazing sheep and cattle, dogs are not allowed at the Reserve.
